"We're not only looking to build a great football club, we want a community, we want somebody to watch games with, to party with, to spread the joy of footy"
Blaise Siefer, Co-Founder of MCFC

Middlebury Club Soccer (MCFC) seeks to provide a competitive playing environment built around community, love of the game, and pickup play style. A brief history: Middlebury College used to have a JV soccer team that encouraged competitive soccer play outside of the Varsity system. When Middlebury eliminated all JV programs in 2019, many of these players had no option outside of IM to play anymore. It was clear that there existed a void.
