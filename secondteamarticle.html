Nov 20, 2021

Midd FC adding Second Travel Team

Due to overwhelming support and demand, Midd FC is adding a second team, spearheaded by Roni Lezama and Charlie Deichman-Caswell. This team will support another 20-30 players and will get more people exposed to competitive play time. We are hoping to formalize exact details in the spring. In the meantime, Roni and Charlie are excited to plan practices on the indoor turf field as it becomes available.
