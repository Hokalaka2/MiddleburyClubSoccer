Meet the Women's Club Soccer Team

Midd FC also consists of an active women's team. The team is currently led by Eleanor Pontikes. Although they only played one game in the fall against dartmouth, they are gearing up for a big spring season.
Currently, the team consists of about 20-30 players but they hope to expand in the coming semesters.

More information can be found by emailing clubsoccer@middlebury.edu or by texting Eleanor Pontikes at (773)456-1111
